Program definition

The new Chemistry and Forensic Sciences program is in line with the state’s scientific direction to develop modern programs geared toward the labor market. The program provides an integrated scientific approach to qualify students for work inside and outside Egypt.

Program message

Preparing distinguished graduates equipped with the necessary skills to keep up with advancements in utilizing modern technology, linking fundamental sciences with forensic evidence. Graduates are also qualified to compete in the local and regional labor markets.
